About Me

The work I have been doing for the last 25 years has been a great teacher, but now I find myself looking to make a change to something more satisfying, something that I can do that will contribute to the wellbeing of others.

I love the prospect of working from home, or having the ability work travel and work. Thats why I think coding and programming, web development etc would be a great place to start.

One of my goals is to create an app for walking meditations. This app will allow people to track their movements while engaging them in ambient meditative tones, binaural beats and nature sounds. I also want to develope a Diary app to make it easy for people to journal but dictation, add photos, daily weather, and general notes. This app will also have a health feature that can track heart rates, steps, and other health metrics.
